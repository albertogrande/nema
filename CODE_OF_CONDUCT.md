# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][cc].

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the
maintainers listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md). All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant 2.1 governs this project and is available at:

[https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/][cc]

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
